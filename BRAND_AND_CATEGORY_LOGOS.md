## Recommended Approach

1. **For Major Brands:** Use official brand websites or trusted logo CDN services
2. **For Local Brands:** Download from official websites or Egyptian retailer sites
3. **For Categories:** Use icon libraries (Flaticon, IconFinder) or create custom icons
4. **Download Format:** PNG with transparent background, minimum 200x200px recommended

## Logo Sources

- **Logos-World.net**: https://logos-world.net/ (High quality brand logos)
- **Wikimedia Commons**: https://commons.wikimedia.org/ (SVG logos)
- **Flaticon**: https://www.flaticon.com/ (Icons and illustrations)
- **IconFinder**: https://www.iconfinder.com/ (Icons and logos)
- **Brand Official Websites**: Always check official brand websites first

## Instructions for Adding Logos

1. Download logos from the URLs above
2. Upload them through the admin panel at `/admin/brands` or `/admin/categories`
3. Or use the API upload endpoint if bulk uploading
4. Ensure logos are:
   - PNG format with transparent background
   - Minimum 200x200px
   - Maximum 2MB file size
   - Square aspect ratio recommended
